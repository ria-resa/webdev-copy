OUTSIDE

RELEASED DATE: October 17, 2024

STARRING:   Sid Lucero, Beauty Gonzales, Marco Masa, Aiden Tyler Patdu

CAST:   James Blanco, Bing Pimentel, Enchong Dee, Joel Torre

OVERVIEW:

A family trying to outrun the zombie outbreak by returning to the father's childhood farmhouse, fortifying it to keep out "The Dead Ones". Alas, there is just as much horror and trauma at the ancestral home as there is outside the gates.

DELETER

RELEASED DATE: : December 25, 2024

STARRING:   Nadine Lustre

CAST:  Louise Delos Reyes, McCoy De Leon, Jeffry Hidalgo, Sarah Jane Abad, Matthew Francisco, Bombi Plata, Keiko Nakajima, Kedebon Colim, Billy Villeta, Charo Laude, Nic Galvez
