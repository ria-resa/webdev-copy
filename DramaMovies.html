DRAMA MOVIE REVIEWS

BIRDSHOT

RELEASED DATE: March 26, 2018

STARRING:   Mary Joy Apostol

CAST:   John Arcilla, Angie Ferro, Arnold Reyes, Manuel Aquino, Ronnie Quizon, Elora Espano, Suzette Ranillo, Ronaldo T. Inocencio, Dido de la Paz, Philmar Quilona, Bangjon Jose, Jason Skyblue, Lowell Conales, Mark Justine, Robin Palmes

OVERVIEW:

"Birdshot" is a mystery-thriller film that tells a story of a young Filipina farm girl who wanders into the boundaries of a Philippine reservation forest. Deep within the reservation she mistakenly shoots and kills a critically endangered and protected Philippine Eagle. As the local authorities begin a manhunt to track down the poacher of a national bird, their investigation leads them to an even more horrific discovery.

LOLO AND THE KID

RELEASED DATE: August 7, 2024

STARRING:   Joel Torre, Euwenn Mikaell

CAST:   Markki Stroem, Shaina Magdayao, Isa Calzado, Meryll Soriano, Joem, Bascon

OVERVIEW:

A hustler and the child he took in routinely con the wealthy - but a life-changing opportunity could end their inseparable bond.

GG (GOOD GAME)

RELEASED DATE: January 24, 2024

STARRING:   Donny Pangilinan

CAST:  Baron Geister, Ronaldo Valdez, Maricel, Laxa, Gold Azeron, Christian Vasques, Boots Anson-Roa, Johannes Rissler

OVERVIEW:

GG, an acronym for "good game," centers around Seth, a determined aspiring gamer who immerses himself in the world of video games who gets into a "competitive barkada gaming" while navigating the various challenges within his own family.

HELLO, LOVE, GOODBYE

RELEASED DATE: December 30, 2019

STARRING:   Kathryn Bernardo, Alden Richards

CAST:   Maymay Entrata, Maricel Laxa, Joross Gamboa, Jameson Blake, Kakai Bautista, Lito Pimentel, Jeffrey Tam, Lovely Abella, William Lorenzo, Maxine Medina, Jerome Ponce, Anthony Jennings.

OVERVIEW:

The film centers on the love story of Joy (Bernardo) and Ethan (Richards), Filipino workers based in Hong Kong. Ethan, a bartender, is keen on romantically pursuing Joy, a domestic helper who is wholly dedicated to providing for her family.

HELLO, LOVE, AGAIN

RELEASED DATE: November 13, 2024

STARRING:   Kathryn Bernardo, Alden Richards

CAST:   Lovely Abella, Jennica Garcia, Maymay Entrata, Jeffrey Tam, Jameson Blake, Valerie, Conception, kakai Bautista, Joross Gamboa, Ruby Rodriguez, Jobert Austria, Mark LaBella

OVERVIEW:

After fighting for their love to conquer time, distance and a global shutdown that keeps them apart, Joy and Ethan meet again in Canada but realize that they have also changed a lot, individually.
