HISTORY MOVIE REVIEWS

QUEZON’S GAME

RELEASED DATE: January 08, 2021

STARRING:   Raymond Bagatsing

CAST:   Rachel Alejandro, Kate Alejandro, Billy Ray, David Bianco, Jennifer Blair-Bianco, Tony Ahn, James Paoleli, Bernard Carritero, Jeremy, Domingo, Gabriel Irabagon, Hamilton, McLeod, Ross Benaby McLeod, Natalia Moon.

OVERVIEW:

In 1938, Philippine President Manuel L. Quezon agrees to welcome Jewish refugees from Germany in the Philippines.

HENERAL LUNA

RELEASED DATE: September 09, 2015

STARRING:   John Arcilla

CAST:   John Arcilla, Mon Confiado, Arron Villaflor, Joem Bascon, Archie Alemania, Epi Quizon, Nonie Buencamino, Paulo Avelino, Leo Martinez, Mylene Dizon, Ronnie Lazaro

OVERVIEW:

The story highlights his conflicts with political leaders, his dedication to the fight for Philippine independence, and the tragic consequences of his uncompromising nature. The film combines action, drama, and a critical examination of loyalty and sacrifice, ultimately portraying Luna as a heroic yet flawed character in the fight for national identity.

GOMBURZA

RELEASED DATE: December 25, 2023

STARRING:   Cedrick Juan, Enchong Dee, Dante Rivero,

CAST:   Piolo Pascual, Elijah Canlas, Dylan ray Talon, Gary Guarino, Tommy Alejandro, Ronnie Lazaro, Elora Espano, Khalil Ramos, Jaime Fabregas, Jeffrey Quizon, Sue Prado, Jomari Angeles, Neil Ryan Sese, Joel Saracho.

OVERVIEW:

Mariano Gomes, José Burgos, and Jacinto Zamora are three brave Filipino Catholic priests whose narrative is told in this stirring and inspirational historical drama.

BONIFACIO: ANG UANG PANGULO

RELEASED DATE: December 25, 2014

STARRING:   Robin Padilla

CAST:   Daniel Padilla, Vina Morales, Eddie Garcia, Jasmine Curtis-Smith, RJ Padilla, Jericho Rosales, Isabel Oli, Joem Bascon, Rommel Padilla, Isko Moreno, Dennis Marasigan, Richard Quan, Junjun Quintana, Cholo Barretto, Miguel Faustmann, Bon Vibar, Jun Nayra, Crispin Pineda, Pepe Herrera, Mike Gayoso, Erlinda Villalobos, Bong Cabrera.

OVERVIEW:

The true story of Andres Bonifacio, a man who rose as a leader in the fight against the Spanish oppressors, and would gain the enmity of even those fighting for the same cause.

GOYO: THE BOY GENERAL

RELEASED DATE: September 5, 2018

STARRING:   Paulo Avelino

CAST:   Rafael Siguion-Reyna, Carlo Aquino, Aaron Villaflor, Gwen Zamora, Mon Confiado, Epy Quizon, Empress Schuck, Alvin Anson, Perla Bautista, Benjamin Alves

OVERVIEW:

The story of Gregorio 'Goyo' del Pilar, one of the youngest Generals during the Philippine-American War who fought in the historic Battle of Tirad Pass.
