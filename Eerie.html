EERIE

RELEASED DATE: March 27, 2019

STARRING:   Bea Alonzo & Charo Santos-Concio

CAST:   Maxene Magalona, Jake Cuenca, Gabby Padilla, Gabby Padilla, Gillian Vicencio, Mary Joy Apostol, Nafa Hilario Cruz, Madeleine Red

GENRE:   Horror

RATINGS:   47% Critique Reviews, 45% Audience Reviews

DIRECTOR:  Mikhail Red

AUTHOR:  Mariah Reodica, Mikhail Red, & Rae Red

OVERVIEW:

When a student's suicide rattles an all-girls Catholic school, a clairvoyant guidance counselor leans on a ghost to uncover the convent's abusive past.
