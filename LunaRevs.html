HENERAL LUNA

RELEASED DATE: September 09, 2015

STARRING:   John Arcilla

CAST:   John Arcilla, Mon Confiado, Arron Villaflor, Joem Bascon, Archie Alemania, Epi Quizon, Nonie Buencamino, Paulo Avelino, Leo Martinez, Mylene Dizon, Ronnie Lazaro

GENRE:   History

RATINGS:   78% Critique Reviews, 88% Audience Reviews

DIRECTOR:  Jerald Tarog

AUTHOR:  Jerald Tarog & E.A Rocha

OVERVIEW:

The story highlights his conflicts with political leaders, his dedication to the fight for Philippine independence, and the tragic consequences of his uncompromising nature. The film combines action, drama, and a critical examination of loyalty and sacrifice, ultimately portraying Luna as a heroic yet flawed character in the fight for national identity.

R C

Told from the 1898 Filipino perspective of the Philippine-American war, this paints a beautiful story of patriotism and country from the non-American view sorely needed. Great performances and a film all Filipino Americans need to watch, even with the historical artistic license. Cinematography is epic. And I appreciate they chose authentic Tagalog dialogue, keeping the heart of the story intact. IMO - THE first successfully made epic period movie in Philippine cinema history hands down.
