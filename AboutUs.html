About Us

PHILikula is a platform dedicated to celebrating Filipino cinema. We believe in the power of Filipino films to tell stories that reflect our culture, struggles, and triumphs. From timeless classics to contemporary works, Philippine cinema captures the heart and resilience of our people. PHILikula features a curated collection of 30 Filipino films—five from each of the following genres: action, comedy, drama, history, horror, and romance

In a world where international content often overshadows local films, PHILikula seeks to support and uplift Filipino movies. Our mission is to offer insightful reviews, critiques, and recommendations that bring Filipino audiences closer to the stories that shape our identity.

Through PHILikula, we aim to bridge the gap between Filipino viewers and the rich diversity of our own film industry. Join us in exploring and appreciating the beauty of Filipino films—themes of family, love, cultural identity, and the strength of the Filipino spirit await.
