LOLO AND THE KID

RELEASED DATE: August 7, 2024

STARRING:   Joel Torre & Euwenn Mikaell

CAST:   Markki Stroem, Shaina Magdayao, Isa Calzado, Meryll Soriano, Joem, Bascon

GENRE:   Drama

RATINGS:   100% Critique Reviews, 88% Audience Reviews

DIRECTOR:  Benedict Mique

AUTHOR:  Benedict Mique

OVERVIEW:

A hustler and the child he took in routinely con the wealthy - but a life-changing opportunity could end their inseparable bond.

Khristine D.

It's a really good drama movie that mixes in some quiet, catchy humor between the lolo and the kid. At first, I was frustrated with how the movie would make me feel sad, then suddenly switch to something funny. But gosh, when you reach the ending, it hits you hard! I couldn't help but break down in tears. JK did a great job stepping into this kind of film, and Sir Joel, as always, was amazing. He’s been such a talented artist for so long, and this movie is just more proof of that!
