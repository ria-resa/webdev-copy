GG (GOOD GAME)

RELEASED DATE: January 24, 2024

STARRING:   Donny Pangilinan

CAST:   Baron Geister, Ronaldo Valdez, Maricel, Laxa, Gold Azeron, Christian Vasques, Boots Anson-Roa, Johannes Rissler

GENRE:   Drama, & Sci-Fi

RATINGS:   70% Critique Reviews, 59% Audience Reviews

DIRECTOR:  Prime Cruz

AUTHOR:  Prime Cruz, Hannah Pangilinan, & Zachary Aquino

OVERVIEW:

GG, an acronym for "good game," centers around Seth, a determined aspiring gamer who immerses himself in the world of video games who gets into a "competitive barkada gaming" while navigating the various challenges within his own family.

Migs Ramos

CinemaBravo

This film is a breath of fresh air, marking the first instance we’ve witnessed such a narrative on our local screens.

He showcases the different phases of Seth’s life effectively and leaves an impression on you. However, in spite of having all the beats to make this truly memorable, it’s the execution that leaves much to be desired in GG (Good Game). It’s a good one-time watch though.
