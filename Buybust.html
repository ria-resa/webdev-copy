BUYBUST

RELEASED DATE: August 1, 2018

STARRING:   Anne Curtis

CAST:   Alex Calleja, Ricky Pascua, Victor Neri, Joross Gamboa, Brandon Vera, Arjo Atayde, Levi Ignacio, Tarek El tayech, ian Ignacio, Sheen Gener, Nafa Hilario, AJ Muhlach, Mara Lopez, Nonie Buencamino, Paolo Rodriguez

GENRE:   Action

RATINGS:   82% Critique Reviews, 66% Audience Reviews

DIRECTOR:  Erik Matti

AUTHOR:  Anton C. Santamaria & Erik Matti

OVERVIEW:

After surviving the slaughter of her entire squad in a drug raid compromised by dirty cops, anti-narcotics special operative Nina Manigan is eager to go head-to-head with the drug cartels that hold a bloody grip on Manila. But when her new mission in the city's most dangerous slum goes south, the angry civilians turn on her squad. Trapped between a brutal drug gang and hordes of bloodthirsty citizens, their only option is to fight their way out.

Late R.

The cinematography is pretty nice in places and the cast is solid, but jeez-louise, it doesn't get a lot else right. The general setup meant it could have been a Filipino ‘The Raid' style film which might have been fun, but unfortunately, there's just too much that goes awry: the characters are extremely thinly written so that you don't actually care for any of them; the fight choreography is atrocious, silly and lacks any realism where most of the performers look like they have no idea what they're doing; and just as badly, even if they did know, you wouldn't have been able to see much of it because the camerawork is all over the place. Those last two are a real problem considering how much of the film consists of just that. To top it all off, it's too long and feels longer because you have to suffer through all of the aforementioned. Ultimately, it's just a badly filmed massacre that is difficult to enjoy which means that ‘Buy Bust' is a bit of a bust.
