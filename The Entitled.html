THE ENTITLED

RELEASED DATE: July 29, 2022

STARRING:   Alex Gonzaga & JC De Vera

CAST:   Ara Mina, Johnny Revilla, Melai Cantiveros, Andrea Abaya, Issa Litton, Tori Garcia, Rolando Inocencio, Felix Petate, Skelly Clarkson, Kobie Brown, Sela Guia, Zabel Lamberth, Louie Kim Sedukis, Marivic B. Ong, Ed Delos Reyes, Humphrey Dela Cruz, Melinda Fabela, Vanny Fabela, Bongjon Jose, Jorge Cabullo

GENRE:   Comedy, Drama, & Romance

RATINGS:   41% Critique Reviews, 14% Audience Reviews

DIRECTOR:  Theodore Boborol

AUTHOR:  Noreen Capili

OVERVIEW:

Unexpectedly dropped into upper-class society, an awkward woman struggles to fit in.

Brian Costello

Common Sense Media

This is a crass comedy that gives new meaning to the word "sophomoric." The Entitled is a Filipino rags-to-riches/romcom that combines elements of, say, The Princess Diaries with more recent comedies that revel in gross-out humor and sex jokes.
