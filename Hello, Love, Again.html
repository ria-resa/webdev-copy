HELLO, LOVE, AGAIN

RELEASED DATE: November 13, 2024

STARRING:   Kathryn Bernardo & Alden Richards

CAST:   Lovely Abella, Jennica Garcia, Maymay Entrata, Jeffrey Tam, Jameson Blake, Valerie, Conception, kakai Bautista, Joross Gamboa, Ruby Rodriguez, Jobert Austria, Mark LaBella

GENRE:   Drama & Romance

RATINGS:   70% Critique Reviews, 97% Audience Reviews

DIRECTOR:  Cathy Garcia-Sampana

AUTHOR:  Carmi Raymundo, Crystal S. San Miguel, & Olivia M. Lamasan

OVERVIEW:

After fighting for their love to conquer time, distance and a global shutdown that keeps them apart, Joy and Ethan meet again in Canada but realize that they have also changed a lot, individually.

Anne Campbell

Film Gate Reviews

Hello, Love, Again has a good idea for the sequel, revisit the same characters in a new city. When Joy leaves Hong Kong for Canada, she’s starting a new life, but with the same love, so romantic issues are sure to abound. I was thinking something like Past Lives, but one where we already knew the characters. Instead the writers took every single idea they had for the sequel and packed all of them in. The result is a movie about everything that says nothing.

Gazettely

As a touching follow-up, “Hello, Love, Again” picks up where Joy and Ethan’s love story left off in the busy streets of Hong Kong and moved to Canada’s cold, difficult environment. Kathryn Bernardo and Alden Richards will be back together in this 2024 movie, following the story that won people over in the 2019 hit “Hello, Love, Goodbye.”

MANILA, Philippines — Blockbuster director Cathy Garcia-Sampana proved once again that doing a sequel from a blockbuster movie will not harm the first one if you are good enough to surpass the pioneer movie. Cathy masterfully did justice to "Hello Love Goodbye," the once top blockbuster movie of all time after Dingdong Dantes and Marian Rivera's Metro Manila Film Festival 2023 entry "Rewind" surpassed it last December.

Lafeyette A.

This is a great conclusion to the original movie called hello love. Goodbye. This is such a fun and heartwarming story and the characters are great, especially the supporting characters to the main character of joy and Ethan. I'm not Filipino but I watched the first one just a few weeks back so I had to see the second one and it's a phenomenal way to continue the story and also close it out with a great feel-good ending. I would recommend this movie to anyone that likes romantic comedies or love stories and I would also recommend watching the first one before you watch this one. And in conclusion, usually sequels aren't any good, but this one is just as good of a movie as the first if not better and in my opinion it is better than the first one.

Alexander Victa

Nothing like seeing a pinoy love story movie in the theater filled with titos and titas reacting to "kilig" scenes! Beautiful story. It was worth the 3-hr drive.

Joshua T.

A more mature take of Hello, Love, Goodbye. There were some minor lapses that could've done better but that's totally fine as the chemistry and acting skills of Kathryn and Alden are just outstanding. The expressive eyes of Alden and that breakdown scene of Kathryn will surely gave them acting nods. My favorite part is the hardship that they experienced during the lockdown, I feel like I was there experiencing it with them. It was just painful to see the world that they dream together are just quickly falling apart. Lastly, Joross and Valerie are scene stealers.
